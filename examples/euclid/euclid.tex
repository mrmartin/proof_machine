\section*{Statement}
\begin{theorem}[euclid]
$$\forall n\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, \exists p\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, ((p > n) \wedge \mathrm{prime}(p))$$
\end{theorem}

\section*{Declared axioms}
The proof cites the following theory-package axioms (trusted assumptions for v0.1):
\begin{itemize}
  \item \texttt{fact\_pos}: $\forall n\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, ((n! + 1) \geq 1)$
  \item \texttt{has\_prime\_divisor}: $\forall m\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, \exists p\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, (\mathrm{prime}(p) \wedge (p \mid m))$
  \item \texttt{euclid\_step}: $\forall p\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, \forall n\!:\!\mathbb{N}.\, ((\mathrm{prime}(p) \wedge (p \mid (n! + 1))) \Rightarrow (p > n))$
\end{itemize}
